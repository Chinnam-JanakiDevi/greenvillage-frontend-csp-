Chinna Turakapalem is a small village located in the Narasaraopeta Mandal of Guntur district in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. Narasaraopeta is a town in the Guntur district and serves as the Mandal headquarters. Here are some details about Chinna Turakapalem:

About Chinna Turakapalem

Location:

Chinna Turakapalem is situated approximately 10 kilometers northeast of Narasaraopeta town. It is surrounded by other neighboring villages and is primarily a rural agricultural area.

Demographics:

The village is home to a population primarily engaged in agricultural activities. The residents are mainly from agrarian backgrounds and are involved in farming practices.

Agriculture:

Like many other villages in the region, agriculture forms the backbone of the economy in Chinna Turakapalem. The fertile land in and around the village supports the cultivation of various crops such as paddy, cotton, groundnuts, and vegetables.

Culture and Festivals:

The village celebrates various cultural and religious festivals, which are an integral part of the community's social life. Festivals such as Sankranti, Ugadi, and Diwali are typically observed with enthusiasm and participation.
