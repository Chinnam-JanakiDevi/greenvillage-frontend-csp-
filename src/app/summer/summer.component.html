MarigoldFlower


Height :6 inches to 3 feet tall.

ph range :7.0 to 7.5.

Soil:Well drained loamy soil

Types:African marigold (Tagetes erecta) and French marigold (Tagetes patula).

SunLight :Full sun, to partial shade

Watering : require watering around once per week

LilyFlower


Height :2–6 ft (60–180 cm).

ph range :6 - 7

Soil: Loamy Soil,Sandy Soil,Clay Soil,Well-Draining Soil

Types:Asiatic Lilies,Oriental Lilies,Trumpet Lilies,Easter Lilies,Daylilies

SunLight : Full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Watering :Lilies require consistent moisture in the soil without overwatering

TomatoPlant


Height :1–3 meters (3–10 ft)

ph range :6.2 to 6.8

Soil: well-drained, sandy or red loam soils rich in organic matter

Types:Cherry,Roma,Better Boy tomatos

SunLight :12 -16 hours of light for the best growing results

Watering :Early in the growing season, watering plants daily in the morning.

Sweet potatoPlant


Height :1-2 inches

ph range :5.5-6.5

Soil:Fertile, well-drained, loose, deep, slightly acidic, fine sandy loam or very sandy soil

Types:Beauregard, Garnet, Jewel, and Purple

SunLight :Full sun requires direct light at least 6 hours/day; prefers 8 - 10 hours/day

Watering :1 inch of water given once a week
